## About

I'm a developer working at the intersection of **data, design, and ease through automation**. My background is in disaster recovery analytics, I spent time at Tetra Tech building Python automation, PowerBI dashboards, and SharePoint tooling for FEMA operations after Hurricane Helene and the Iowa winter storms. These days I'm focused on shipping software: APIs, learning tools, and the occasional generative-art experiment.

Outside of code: guitar (midwest emo / math rock), Ableton, p5.js flow fields, Pokémon TCG (Tomokazu Komiya bias), and a small but growing collection of *Trichocereus* cultivars.
